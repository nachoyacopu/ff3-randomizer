Known issues
  • Saving in the Altar Cave before beating the Land Turtle, and reloading this save will drop you next to Argass Castle, most certainly blocking your progress - start a new save if this happens
  • Gold chests only have their gold amount randomized
  • Welcome text is bugged in some shops
  • Speed of your characters in battle is unchanged when in mini form
